Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about air duct cleaning, dryer vent cleaning and our Suffern, NY services.

What is air duct cleaning and why does it matter?

Air duct cleaning is the process of removing accumulated dust, debris, allergens and contaminants from your HVAC system's ductwork. Over time, these pollutants build up and recirculate through your home's air, causing allergy symptoms, poor air quality and reduced HVAC efficiency. Professional cleaning significantly improves the air you breathe every day.

How often should I have my air ducts cleaned?

Most experts recommend air duct cleaning every 3 to 5 years for average households. However, homes with pets, allergy or asthma sufferers, recent renovations, or high dust levels may benefit from more frequent cleaning. We can inspect your system and give you an honest recommendation based on its actual condition.

Why is dryer vent cleaning important?

Clogged dryer vents are a leading cause of home fires in the United States. Lint and debris accumulate inside the vent over time, restricting airflow and creating dangerous heat buildup. Regular dryer vent cleaning eliminates this fire hazard, reduces drying times, lowers energy bills and extends the life of your dryer significantly.

Do you offer commercial air duct cleaning in Rockland County?

Yes, we provide full commercial air duct cleaning and HVAC duct cleaning services for offices, retail spaces, restaurants and commercial buildings throughout Suffern and all of Rockland County, NY. A clean commercial HVAC system means healthier air for your employees and customers, and better system efficiency all year long.

How long does an air duct cleaning service take?

A typical residential air duct cleaning service takes between 2 and 4 hours depending on the size of your home and the condition of your ductwork. Our technicians work efficiently and cleanly, protecting your floors and furniture throughout the process and leaving your home spotless when the job is done.
